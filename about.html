About Us

Our Story

Oseph Group limited is a 100% employee-owned construction firm providing integrated construction services through a full range of delivery methods. We excel at projects of all sizes from apartments,maisonattes,bungalows,Underground and suspended water tanks and much more. We know what it takes to get things built and innovative solutions, talented people and specialized equipment top the list. But ensuring a successful project from start to finish takes even more industry-leading safety practices, a multi-disciplined in-house estimating team, extensive self-perform capabilities and a fully integrated Special Projects Group, to name a few. Oseph Group has all this, and more, to ensure a seamless, cost-efficient process along the way and a satisfied client at the end.

Background

Oseph Group limited began with the construction of a pit latrine at a homestead in Nanyuki.We started with a dream, threw in a lot of grit and determination, and eventually grew into a thriving 100% employee-owned company that is consistently ranked among the most Reliable top 100 construction firms in the Nation. Guided by our rich history, our exceptional teams build with passion, which makes us one of the very best in our industry.
